Make the workbench feel more like Notes

Today 13:42 Edited just now ALL-IN-ONE / design preview
Process stays visible, but it should read like a margin note instead of a separate tool widget.

What to reduce

Pull back on bubbles, chips, and panel-within-panel framing. Let the app still feel like a desktop tool, but make the center read as one calm document surface.

What to keep

Keep the existing functionality and pane structure. The change should come from layout rhythm, iconography, spacing, and quieter companion UI.

Assistant recommendation

The AI area should feel like a native writing companion: inline suggestions in the document, and a right sidebar that behaves more like Notes attachments and smart actions than a standalone chat canvas.

Visual principles

  • turn text-heavy controls into icon-led affordances wherever the meaning stays obvious
  • use larger margins and a narrower reading column for the central document
  • keep color as a restrained accent, not a dominant surface
